The Link Journey

20 Years of Milestones

2005
World's Largest REIT IPO
Link Asset Management lists on HKEX with 180 properties from Hong Kong Housing Authority. The largest REIT IPO in history at the time, raising HK$21.8B.
2010
First Asset Enhancement Initiative
Pioneering AEI programme launches, transforming ageing public housing malls into vibrant community retail destinations.
2014
First Overseas Acquisition
Link Asset Management makes its first offshore move, beginning the APAC diversification strategy that would define the next decade.
2017
ESG Framework Formalised
Comprehensive sustainability strategy adopted with Board-level accountability. Climate risk integrated into investment decisions.
2020
Singapore & Australia Entry
Jurong Point acquisition marks Singapore entry. Australian office portfolio expanded. APAC footprint now covers 4 key markets.
2022
Net-Zero 2035 Commitment
Board commits to Scope 1 & 2 net-zero by 2035. Internal carbon pricing explored. SBTi-aligned interim targets for 2030 set.
2024
Asia's Largest REIT
Portfolio reaches HK$225.8B across 154 properties in 5 markets. GRESB 87/100. 19 consecutive years of DPU growth.
2025–26
20th Anniversary Year
A year of celebration, reflection and forward commitment — honouring communities, unitholders and people who built Link Asset Management.

Link Asset Management is one of the largest private solar PV operators in Hong Kong, with 4.5 MW installed across 53 assets generating more than 4,200 MWh annually — directly reducing purchased electricity costs and Scope 2 emissions.

In Mainland China, rooftop PV systems are being deployed across three logistics centres with a 17 MW pipeline underway.

HVAC & Smart Controls

4.37%EMS Annual Saving HK
54 AssetsHK EMS Coverage
10 ChillersLife-cycle Replaced

The Energy Management System (EMS), built on 15 years of optimisation work, is active across all 54 Hong Kong retail assets. AI-driven analytics, smart controls and predictive maintenance reduce downtime and keep energy intensity falling year on year.

Chiller upgrades, HVAC improvements and system integration are standard in every enhancement plan. Solar films and high-reflectivity roof coatings pilot passive heat load reduction.

LED replacement programmes and control system automation — occupancy sensors, daylight harvesting, time-based scheduling — are standard in all asset enhancement plans. Motion-sensor lighting in car parks delivered a 25.2% ROI.

3,109 EV charging points are now deployed across Hong Kong, exceeding targets and future-proofing assets for shifting tenant mobility needs.

Waste & Circular Economy

12,208 TWaste Diverted HK
27.0%General Waste Recycled
HK$4.4MPotential Annual Saving
33%Efficiency Improvement

Link Asset Management diverted 12,208 tonnes from disposal in Hong Kong in 2024/25 — a 27.0% general waste recovery rate, up from 20.2%. This represents HK$4.4M in potential annual cost savings under Hong Kong's MSW charging scheme.

Green leases and co-investment with tenants drive mutual performance gains. 33% efficiency improvement achieved through lease and facility standardisation.

Retail occupancy remains exceptionally strong across all markets, demonstrating that sustainably managed assets attract and retain quality tenants.
